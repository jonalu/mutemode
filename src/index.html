Mute Mode is a norwegian electronic music duo consisting of Mats Systaddal and John Trygve Mulhearn Evensen. Their music is tough and raw and the visual stage show, together with the repetitive riff, makes for a hypnotic and physical experience. The Aggressive synths, samplers, drum machines and perc provide driving and dirty rhythms.
